       - Tourist volume estimate (reuse estimateTouristVolume from crowd-colors.ts)

       - FilteredMap showing events for this destination+month
       - EventCardGrid with all events happening at this destination during this month

       - Direct Booking.com CTA button pre-filled with destination and month dates
       - "Book your stay in {destination} for {month}" — prominent, conversion-focused per user decision
